ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

This website is my final project for Harvard's CS50 Introduction to Computer Science. I wanted to play a bit with APIs and learn more about HTML and CSS.

Creating this website allowed me to do both. I had the chance to integrate two different APIs: Open Notify and Google Maps. The former allows me to retrieve, among other information, the coordinates of the ISS. Which I'm then passing to the Google Maps API call.
